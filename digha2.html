Digha

The Seaside Paradise of West Bengal

About Digha

Digha is a popular seaside resort town in the state of West Bengal, India. Known for its pristine beaches, casuarina plantations, and tranquil atmosphere, Digha offers a perfect getaway for those seeking sun, sand, and sea. With its shallow sand beach, Digha is an ideal destination for both relaxation and water activities.

Key Attractions

Digha Beach

Enjoy the golden sands and calm waters of the main Digha Beach, perfect for sunbathing and swimming.

Marine Aquarium

Explore the diverse marine life of the Bay of Bengal at the Digha Marine Aquarium and Research Centre.

Chandaneswar Temple

Visit the ancient Chandaneswar Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, located near Digha.

Plan Your Visit

  • Enjoy sunbathing and swimming at Digha Beach
  • Take a stroll along the Casuarina plantations
  • Visit the New Digha Beach for water sports activities
  • Explore the Marine Aquarium and Research Centre
  • Watch the sunset at Amarabati Park
  • Take a day trip to nearby Mandarmani or Tajpur beaches
  • Visit the Chandaneswar Temple during Chaitra Sankranti

Best Time to Visit

Digha can be visited throughout the year, but each season offers a unique experience:

  • Winter (October to February): Ideal weather for beach activities and outdoor exploration
  • Summer (March to June): Hot weather, but less crowded beaches and lower accommodation rates
  • Monsoon (July to September): Lush green surroundings, but some beach activities may be restricted

The peak tourist season is from October to February when the weather is most pleasant for beach activities.

Local Cuisine

Digha offers a variety of delicious Bengali and seafood dishes. Don't miss trying:

  • Fresh seafood dishes like Pomfret fry and Prawn curry
  • Bengali sweets like Rosogolla and Mishti Doi
  • Local snacks like Jhalmuri and Ghugni
  • Traditional Bengali thali meals
  • Coconut water from local vendors on the beach
