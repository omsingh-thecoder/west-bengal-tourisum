Mandarmani

Relax on the pristine beaches of Mandarmani, a tranquil coastal retreat in West Bengal.

About Mandarmani

Mandarmani is a serene beach destination located in the East Midnapore district of West Bengal. Known for its long, uninterrupted stretch of sandy beach and calm waters, Mandarmani offers a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life. The beach is ideal for relaxation, beach activities, and enjoying beautiful sunsets.

Key Attractions

Mandarmani Beach

Enjoy the serene beauty of Mandarmani Beach, known for its clean sands and tranquil atmosphere. Perfect for sunbathing, swimming, and beach games.

Beach Sunset

Witness stunning sunsets over the Bay of Bengal from the shores of Mandarmani Beach. The view is breathtaking and offers a perfect end to your day.

Sea Activities

Engage in various sea activities such as boating, jet-skiing, and beach volleyball. Mandarmani's calm waters make it ideal for water sports.

Plan Your Visit

  • Relax on the pristine beach and enjoy beach activities
  • Watch the stunning sunset over the Bay of Bengal
  • Explore local seafood delicacies at beachside shacks
  • Take a boat ride or engage in water sports

Timings and Entry Fee

Mandarmani is accessible throughout the day and does not have specific entry fees. However, some activities or private beach resorts may have their own timings and charges, so it's advisable to check in advance.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Mandarmani is from October to March. Here’s a seasonal breakdown:

  • Autumn (October to November): Pleasant weather with cool breezes makes it ideal for beach outings and water activities.
  • Winter (December to February): Cool temperatures and clear skies are perfect for relaxing on the beach and enjoying outdoor activities.
  • Summer (March to June): Hot and humid weather may be uncomfortable for beach activities, but early mornings and evenings can be enjoyable.
  • Monsoon (July to September): Heavy rainfall and rough seas can disrupt travel plans and make beach activities less enjoyable.
