Kalimpong

Discover the scenic beauty and cultural richness of Kalimpong, a charming hill station in West Bengal.

About Kalimpong

Kalimpong is a picturesque hill station located in the Darjeeling district of West Bengal. Nestled in the eastern Himalayas, it offers stunning views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Known for its pleasant climate and vibrant cultural heritage, Kalimpong is a popular destination for tourists seeking both relaxation and adventure.

Key Attractions

Scenic Views

Marvel at the breathtaking views of the Himalayas and the lush landscapes surrounding Kalimpong.

Monasteries

Explore the serene monasteries in Kalimpong, which offer a glimpse into the region's rich Buddhist culture.

Local Markets

Experience the vibrant local markets where you can shop for unique handicrafts and local products.

Nature Trails

Embark on nature trails to explore the rich flora and fauna of the region, and enjoy the peaceful surroundings.

Plan Your Visit

  • Visit the famous monasteries like Zang Dhok Palri Phodang and Tharpa Choling
  • Stroll through the local markets and sample regional delicacies
  • Enjoy nature walks and hikes in the surrounding hills and forests
  • Relax at one of the many scenic viewpoints

Timings and Entry Fee

Kalimpong is a town and does not have specific opening hours or entry fees. It is accessible throughout the day. However, specific attractions such as monasteries may have their own visiting hours, so it's advisable to check in advance.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kalimpong is from March to June and September to December. Here’s a seasonal breakdown:

  • Spring (March to June): Pleasant weather and blooming flowers make it ideal for sightseeing.
  • Monsoon (July to September): Heavy rainfall can cause landslides and disrupt travel plans.
  • Winter (October to December): Cool and comfortable weather, perfect for exploring.
