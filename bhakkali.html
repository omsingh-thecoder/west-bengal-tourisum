Bhakkhali

A hidden gem of West Bengal, Bhakkhali offers an off-the-beaten-path experience with its serene landscapes and tranquil environment.

About Bhakkhali

Bhakkhali is a quaint village in the South 24 Parganas district of West Bengal. Known for its tranquil environment and beautiful landscapes, Bhakkhali is an ideal destination for those looking to escape the hustle and bustle of city life. The village is surrounded by lush greenery and offers a peaceful retreat for nature enthusiasts and travelers.

Key Attractions

Scenic Views

Enjoy the serene and picturesque landscapes that Bhakkhali has to offer, with its lush greenery and tranquil environment.

Riverside

Relax by the riverside and experience the calm and soothing ambiance of Bhakkhali’s natural surroundings.

Local Culture

Explore the local culture and traditions of Bhakkhali, and immerse yourself in the authentic rural experience.

Plan Your Visit

  • Stroll through the village and enjoy the scenic beauty
  • Visit local markets and experience the traditional lifestyle

  • Explore nearby natural attractions and enjoy outdoor activities

Timings and Entry Fee

Bhakkhali is a village and does not have specific opening hours or entry fees. It is accessible throughout the day. However, it is advisable to check local conditions or with local authorities for any updates before planning your visit.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bhakkhali is from October to March. Here’s a seasonal breakdown:

  • Winter (October to March): Pleasant weather, ideal for exploring and outdoor activities
  • Summer (April to June): Hot temperatures; best to visit early in the morning or late afternoon
  • Monsoon (July to September): Heavy rainfall may affect travel plans
