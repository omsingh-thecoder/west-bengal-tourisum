Popular Tourist Spots

Bakkhali Beach

A pristine 7 km long beach with golden sand and clear blue waters, perfect for relaxation and water activities.

Henry's Island

A picturesque island known for its mangrove forests, fish farms, and serene beaches.

Crocodile Park

A unique park where you can observe crocodiles in their natural habitat and learn about conservation efforts.

Jambu Dwip

An uninhabited island accessible by boat, offering a glimpse of untouched nature and diverse wildlife.
