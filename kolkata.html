Welcome to Kolkata

The City of Joy

About Kolkata

Kolkata, formerly known as Calcutta, is the capital of West Bengal and a vibrant metropolis with a rich cultural heritage. Known as the "City of Joy," Kolkata is famous for its colonial architecture, intellectual pursuits, and mouth-watering cuisine.

Top Attractions

Victoria Memorial

A magnificent marble building dedicated to Queen Victoria, now serving as a museum.

Howrah Bridge

An iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River, connecting Kolkata and Howrah.

Park Street

A popular street known for its restaurants, nightlife, and colonial-era architecture.

Things to Do

  • Take a heritage walk through the colonial areas
  • Visit the Indian Museum, the oldest and largest in India
  • Explore the bustling New Market for shopping
  • Enjoy a boat ride on the Hooghly River
  • Savor traditional Bengali cuisine at local eateries

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kolkata is from October to March when the weather is pleasant and dry. This period also coincides with major festivals like Durga Puja, making it an ideal time to experience the city's vibrant culture.
