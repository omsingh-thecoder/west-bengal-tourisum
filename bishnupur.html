Bishnupur

Explore the historic town of Bishnupur, known for its terracotta temples and rich cultural heritage.

About Bishnupur

Bishnupur, located in the Bankura district of West Bengal, is renowned for its exquisite terracotta temples and rich cultural history. The town's temples, dating back to the 17th century, showcase intricate terracotta craftsmanship depicting mythological themes. Bishnupur also serves as a hub for the traditional art of Baluchari silk weaving.

Key Attractions

Terracotta Temples

Marvel at the stunning terracotta temples of Bishnupur, such as the Jor Bangla and Keshta Rai Temples, famous for their intricate carvings.

Historical Architecture

Discover the unique architectural style of Bishnupur, characterized by its blend of traditional and regional design elements.

Baluchari Silk Weaving

Explore the traditional art of Baluchari silk weaving, known for its intricate patterns and vibrant colors.

Plan Your Visit

  • Visit the terracotta temples like Jor Bangla and Keshta Rai
  • Explore the local markets for traditional Baluchari silk sarees
  • Walk around the historic town to appreciate its unique architecture
  • Attend local festivals and cultural events to experience the town's heritage

Timings and Entry Fee

Bishnupur is a town and does not have specific opening hours or entry fees. It is accessible throughout the day. However, specific attractions such as temples may have their own visiting hours, so it's advisable to check in advance.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Bishnupur is from October to March. Here’s a seasonal breakdown:

  • Autumn (October to November): Pleasant weather and clear skies make it ideal for sightseeing.
  • Winter (December to February): Cool and comfortable weather, perfect for exploring the town and its attractions.
  • Summer (March to June): Hot temperatures may make sightseeing less comfortable, but early mornings and evenings can be pleasant.
  • Monsoon (July to September): Heavy rainfall can cause inconvenience and disrupt travel plans.
