Popular Tourist Spots

Rasmancha Temple

A unique pyramid-shaped temple with intricate terracotta work, built in 1600 AD.

Jor Bangla Temple

A twin-hut styled temple with elaborate terracotta panels depicting scenes from the Ramayana and Mahabharata.

Dalmadal Cannon

A massive cannon from the 17th century, symbolizing the military might of the Malla kings.

Bishnupur Silk

Famous for its Baluchari sarees, featuring intricate designs inspired by mythological scenes.
