Popular Tourist Spots

Tiger Hill

Famous for its stunning sunrise views of the Kanchenjunga and the eastern Himalayas.

Batasia Loop

A spiral railway track where the toy train takes a 360-degree turn, offering panoramic views.

Happy Valley Tea Estate

One of the oldest tea gardens in Darjeeling, offering tours and tea tasting experiences.

Padmaja Naidu Himalayan Zoological Park

Home to rare Himalayan species like the red panda and snow leopard.
