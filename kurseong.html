Kurseong

Known as the 'Land of White Orchids,' Kurseong is a picturesque hill station with lush tea gardens and stunning views.

About Kurseong

Kurseong is a serene hill station located in the Darjeeling district of West Bengal, often referred to as the 'Land of White Orchids.' Nestled at an altitude of around 1,458 meters, Kurseong offers a peaceful retreat amidst its lush tea gardens, pleasant weather, and beautiful views of the Himalayas. It is a popular destination for those seeking tranquility and a connection with nature.

Key Attractions

Tea Gardens

Explore the extensive tea gardens that surround Kurseong and learn about tea production while enjoying the scenic views.

Scenic Views

Experience breathtaking views of the Himalayas and the surrounding valleys from various viewpoints around Kurseong.

St. Joseph's Church

Visit this historic church, known for its beautiful architecture and peaceful surroundings.

Monasteries

Discover the tranquil monasteries in Kurseong and experience the serene ambiance of these spiritual retreats.

Plan Your Visit

  • Stroll through the tea gardens and enjoy tea tasting
  • Visit local viewpoints for panoramic views of the Himalayas
  • Explore historic sites like St. Joseph's Church and local monasteries
  • Relax in the serene environment and enjoy the cool climate

Timings and Entry Fee

Timings and entry fees may vary for different attractions in Kurseong.

  • Tea Gardens: Open daily; timings may vary
  • View Points: Generally open throughout the day
  • St. Joseph's Church and Monasteries: Generally open throughout the day

Note: It is advisable to check with local authorities or tour operators for specific timings and entry fees for each attraction.

Best Time to Visit

The best time to visit Kurseong is from March to June and September to November. Here’s a seasonal breakdown:

  • Spring (March to June): Pleasant weather, ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities
  • Monsoon (July to August): Heavy rainfall may affect travel plans
  • Autumn (September to November): Clear skies and pleasant temperatures
  • Winter (December to February): Cold temperatures; still a good time if you enjoy cooler climates
