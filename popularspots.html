Popular Tourist Spots

Explore the best of West Bengal

Darjeeling

Famous hill station known for its tea plantations and scenic beauty.

Location: Northern West Bengal

Sundarbans

World's largest mangrove forest and home to the Royal Bengal Tiger.

Location: Southern West Bengal

Victoria Memorial

Iconic marble building in Kolkata dedicated to Queen Victoria.

Location: Kolkata

Howrah Bridge

Iconic cantilever bridge over the Hooghly River in Kolkata.

Location: Kolkata

Digha Beach

Popular seaside resort town with beautiful beaches.

Location: East Midnapore District

Santiniketan

University town founded by Rabindranath Tagore, known for its cultural heritage.

Location: Birbhum District

Murshidabad

Historical city famous for its Hazarduari Palace and Nawabi heritage.

Location: Murshidabad District

Mandarmani

Seaside resort village known for its long driveable beach.

Location: East Midnapore District

Bishnupur

Ancient town famous for its terracotta temples and Baluchari sarees.

Location: Bankura District

Kalimpong

Hill station known for its panoramic views and Buddhist monasteries.

Location: Kalimpong District

Bakkhali

Serene beach town with a unique crescent-shaped beach.

Location: South 24 Parganas District

Kurseong

Hill station known as the "Land of White Orchids" with tea gardens.

Location: Darjeeling District

Mirik

Picturesque hill station with a beautiful lake and tea gardens.

Location: Darjeeling District

Dooars

Region known for its wildlife sanctuaries and tea gardens.

Location: Jalpaiguri and Alipurduar Districts

Gangasagar

Sacred island where the Ganges meets the Bay of Bengal.

Location: South 24 Parganas District

New Town Eco Park

Large urban park in Kolkata with various attractions and activities.

Location: New Town, Kolkata
