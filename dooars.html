Popular Tourist Spots

Gorumara National Park

Home to the Indian one-horned rhinoceros, elephants, and various bird species.

Jaldapara National Park

Known for its rich biodiversity and the largest population of Indian one-horned rhinoceros in West Bengal.

Buxa Tiger Reserve

A haven for wildlife enthusiasts, offering a chance to spot tigers, leopards, and various other animals.

Dooars Tea Gardens

Vast stretches of lush green tea plantations, offering picturesque views and tea tasting experiences.
