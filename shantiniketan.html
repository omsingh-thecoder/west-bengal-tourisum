Cultural Attractions

Visva-Bharati University

Founded by Rabindranath Tagore, this open-air university is the heart of Shantiniketan's cultural legacy.

Uttarayan Complex

A collection of houses where Tagore lived, now converted into museums showcasing his life and works.

Kala Bhavana

The art college of Visva-Bharati, known for its contribution to contemporary Indian art.

Poush Mela Ground

The venue for the famous Poush Mela, an annual fair celebrating Bengali folk culture and handicrafts.
